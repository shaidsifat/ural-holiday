Payment Policy

WE PROTECT YOUR DATA AND DO NOT SHARE IT WITH THIRD PARTIES

We accept all major credit & debit cards issued globally.

The card holder must be one of the passengers for an online payment.

Please note fares are guaranteed only upon ticketing and not upon submission of payment.

If there is any issue with your payments we will notify you within 24 hours.

Please note all hotel, car rental and tour bookings are only confirmed upon delivery of complete confirmation details to the email you provided with your reservation. In some cases prepayment maybe required to receive confirmation.

We Dont accept third party Credit card or debit card payments.

Please navigate to the link in the payment verification email that we sent you. Here you will be able to upload the additional documentation that allows us to verify that the cardholder approved the transaction to purchase your ticket. Please ensure to comply with the size limitations of the documents you are requested to upload.

Once you have uploaded your documentation, you will receive an automated response acknowledging the receipt of information. Later on, you will then receive another email confirming the status of the verification

You will be required to send the following details to epayments@uralholidays.co.uk when requested.
